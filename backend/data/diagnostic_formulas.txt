Predictive Maintenance Diagnostic Formula Reference
=================================================

The equations below mirror the current logic used in the application for mechanical,
electrical, thermal, anomaly, and health calculations.

1. Vibration RMS and Overall Velocity
   RMS_axis = sqrt(mean(a^2))
   RMS_overall = sqrt(RMSx^2 + RMSy^2 + RMSz^2)

2. Peak and Crest Factor
   Peak_axis = max(abs(a))
   Crest_Factor = Peakx / RMSx

3. Kurtosis for Bearing Damage Detection
   Kurtosis = E[(x - mu)^4] / sigma^4
   Elevated kurtosis indicates impulsive behavior that is typical of early bearing pitting or cracking.

4. FFT Magnitude and Dominant Frequency
   Magnitude(f) = (2 / N) * |FFT(signal - mean(signal))|
   Dominant_Frequency = frequency[argmax(Magnitude(f), f > 0)]

5. Bearing Health Probability
   P_bearing = clamp((max(kurtosis_x, kurtosis_y) / 4.0) + (RMS_overall / 15.0), 0.02, 0.95)

6. Imbalance Probability
   P_imbalance = clamp(RMS_overall / 8.0, 0.05, 0.90)

7. Electrical Apparent Power and Efficiency
   P_theoretical = V * I * sqrt(3)
   Efficiency(%) = clamp((P_active / P_theoretical) * 100, 0, 100)

8. Load Percentage
   Load(%) = (P_active / 10.0) * 100
   Note: the current implementation assumes a nominal 10 kW motor.

9. Power Factor
   PF = P_active / P_apparent

10. Energy and Frequency Monitoring
    Energy_cumulative = sum(P_active * delta_t)
    Delta_f = |f_measured - f_nominal|

11. Thermal Rule Thresholds
    Warning if Temperature > 70 C
    Critical if Temperature > 85 C

12. Anomaly Score
    Score_anomaly = clamp(1 - (decision_function(x) + 0.5), 0, 1)
    Here x is the feature vector used by the Isolation Forest model.

13. Aggregate Health Score
    Health = 100 - penalties(alert severity, alert count, RMS_overall)

14. ISO 10816 Zone Approximation
    Zone A: RMS_overall <= 1.12 mm/s
    Zone B: RMS_overall <= 2.8 mm/s
    Zone C: RMS_overall <= 7.1 mm/s
    Zone D: RMS_overall > 7.1 mm/s

15. AI Analysis Business Metrics
    Efficiency_Gain(%) = ((Baseline Energy - Current Energy) / Baseline Energy) * 100
    Risk_Reduction(%) = ((Baseline Risk - Residual Risk) / Baseline Risk) * 100
    Component_Risk(%) = weighted(vibration, thermal, electrical, historical alerts)
